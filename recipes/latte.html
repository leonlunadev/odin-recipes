Lavender Latte

Description

The lavender latte is a refreshing seasonal drink that is perfect for fall weather.

Lavender lattes are delicious hot or cold.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 3 table spoons fresh or dried lavender flowers
  • 2 cups sugar
  • Espresso
  • Whole milk

Steps:

  1. Bring water and lavender to a boil in a sauce pan
  2. Reduce heat and stir in sugar until fully dissolved
  3. Simmer for about 15 minutes
  4. Remove from the heat and allow to cool and steep for at least 1 hour or longer, until the flavor is to your liking. Strain out the lavender. Pour the lavender syrup into a bottle and keep in the refrigerator. Mix the syrup into your favorite drinks and enjoy.
  5. Make double shot espresso and mix with simple syrup
  6. Steam or froth milk
  7. Pour the steamed/frothed milk into lavender espresso
