Lavender Infused Lemonade

Description

Lavender Lemonade is a refreshing drink for a summer day.

Lavender lemonade's aesthetics will impress your friends at a summer party

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups water
  • 3 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup dried lavender
  • 4 cups fresh lemon juice (16-20 lemons)
  • Ice for serving
  • lemon slices and fresh lavender sprigs for garnish

Steps:

  1. Bring water and lavender to a boil in a sauce pan
  2. Reduce heat and stir in sugar until fully dissolved
  3. Simmer for about 15 minutes
  4. Remove from the heat and allow to cool and steep for at least 1 hour or longer, until the flavor is to your liking. Strain out the lavender. Pour the lavender syrup into a bottle and keep in the refrigerator. Mix the syrup into your favorite drinks and enjoy.
  5. Mix together lavender syrup and lemon juice
  6. Serve over ice
  7. Garnish with fresh lavender and lemon slices
