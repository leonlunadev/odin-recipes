Lavendar Infused Lemonade

Description

Creamy chicken pesto pasta is a delightful fusion of rich, creamy flavors and vibrant Mediterranean-inspired ingredients. This dish combines tender pieces of chicken with al dente pasta, all enveloped in a luscious creamy pesto sauce that's as visually appealing as it is delicious. The key to this delectable creation is the aromatic basil pesto, which infuses the dish with its signature herbal and nutty notes. The creamy sauce, often made with a combination of heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and a touch of butter, complements the pesto's intensity, creating a velvety and indulgent backdrop for the pasta. As the chicken simmers in this harmonious medley, it absorbs the pesto's vibrant green hue and herbal essence, resulting in a mouthwatering blend of savory, aromatic, and creamy elements.

This dish is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes, with the vivid green pesto contrasting beautifully against the white of the creamy sauce and the golden-brown seared chicken. The final touch typically involves tossing cooked pasta into the creamy pesto sauce, ensuring that every bite is a balanced symphony of flavors and textures. Whether enjoyed as a cozy weeknight dinner or served to impress guests, creamy chicken pesto pasta is a tantalizing creation that brings a taste of the Mediterranean to your table, offering a creamy, dreamy, and utterly satisfying culinary experience.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces (about 2 cups) of your favorite pasta (e.g., penne, fettuccine, or linguine)
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2/3 cup basil pesto sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Begin by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set it aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, season the chicken breasts with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.
  4. In the same skillet, add the basil pesto sauce, heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, and unsalted butter. Stir the sauce over medium heat until the butter is melted, and the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the cooked pasta and sliced chicken to the skillet, and gently toss them in the creamy pesto sauce to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat everything through.
  6. Just before serving, add the halved cherry tomatoes and cook for a minute or two until they start to soften slightly.
  7. Divide the creamy chicken pesto pasta among serving plates, garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired, and serve immediately.
